01 Strategy

Developer Acquisition Funnel

A progressive 5-stage pipeline that filters broad community outreach into a curated group of industry-ready Sui builders.

1
Outreach
Broad reach across 10 countries through university clubs, blockchain conferences, and partner networks. DeVinci Blockchain (12+ antennas), KryptoSphere, BSA, W3N, DSN, ETH Blockchain Club, EPFL Blockchain Club.
2
Certification Workshops
18 two-day in-person workshops. First quality filter via Move fundamentals certification. Verifies baseline skill and commitment level.
3
Hackathons
5 week-long hackathons across UK, Italy, Estonia, Spain, Switzerland. Curation by execution: real technical output surfaces the strongest builders.
4
Bootcamps
5 ten-day intensive bootcamps with Mysten Labs engineers. Advanced training in security, indexing, and infrastructure.
5
Incubator
Long-term ecosystem engagement through bounties, employment referrals, and project incubation. Graduates become researchers, industry builders, Mysten employees, or found their own Sui projects.

Sep — Nov 2026
Phase 1: Certification Blitz
12 of 18 certifications run in Q1, roughly one every 7 days. This front-loading maximizes the pipeline going into hackathon season. The mobile workforce rotates across countries.
Nov 2026 — Jan 2027
Phase 2: Hackathon Season
5 hackathons bridge Q1 and Q2. Estonia, UK, and Switzerland hackathons run in Nov-Dec while certifications are still completing. Italy and Spain hackathons follow in January.
Jan — Feb 2027
Phase 3: Bootcamp Intensive
5 bootcamps in 5 weeks closes Q2. Mysten Labs engineers deliver advanced instruction on security, indexing, and infrastructure. Participants are pre-curated through certifications and hackathons.
